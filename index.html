Our Mission

At JR SITE LLC, we are the evolution of decades of hands-on experience in Puerto Rico’s infrastructure industry. Born as a family company with deep roots in asphalt paving and earthwork, we have grown into one of the island’s largest and most trusted construction firms. Our mission is to build a stronger, better Puerto Rico for current and future generations while creating meaningful opportunities for new talent to rise and showcase their potential.
